Wine Key

A Solution to Cross-Company Shared Inventories

We developed this application at the request of Fire&Vine, a company best known for their El Gaucho locations across the greater Seattle area. With this app, a user can view wine inventories for stores across the company and locate excess bottles that may not be sold otherwise. Management can update their store inventory at the click of a button, and quickly contact locations they KNOW have the wine they need without having to call around.

Wavel

Wavel - where your travel inquiries meet weather forecasts, best restaurants to try, and hotel availabilities in a single search. This was my first team project in UW's Bootcamp. With Wavel, a user can search for a city by name, and a series of web APIs gather information to return a five-day forecast, top-rated restaurants, and available hotels in that city, as well as an interactive map of the area.

Burger Builders!

Burger Builders is a fun little page where a user can enter a description of a hamburger they'd like to eat. Their burger can be moved back and forth between a ready-to-eat column and a consumed column, or deleted from the page. This project utilizes a mySQL database to persistently store user's burger information.
